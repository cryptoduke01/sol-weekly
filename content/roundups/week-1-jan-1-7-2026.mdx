# Week 1: January 1-7, 2026

Welcome to the first edition of Solana Weekly Roundup! This week has been packed with exciting developments across the ecosystem.

## Top Stories

### Jupiter Launches Perpetuals Exchange

Jupiter unveiled its highly anticipated perpetuals trading platform this week, marking a significant milestone for DeFi on Solana. The new platform offers:

- Zero-slippage trading
- Up to 50x leverage
- Deep liquidity pools
- Competitive funding rates

Early users have reported smooth trading experiences, with over $100M in volume in the first 24 hours.

### Jito Surpasses $2B TVL

The liquid staking protocol reached a new milestone this week, crossing $2 billion in Total Value Locked. This achievement solidifies Jito's position as one of the leading liquid staking solutions on Solana.

Key highlights:

- Growing validator network
- Enhanced MEV rewards distribution
